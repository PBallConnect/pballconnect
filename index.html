Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment to You
PBallConnect was built by pickleball players, for pickleball players. We collect only what we need to connect you with others who want to play. We will never sell your data, never use it for advertising, and you can delete your account at any time.
What We Collect
And why we need it:
First Name + Nickname
So other players can identify you in the app
Last Name initial (optional)
For your player card display only
Email address
Required to sign in and receive match invites
Phone number (optional)
For future match notifications and score recording
Age range (e.g. 41–45)
For community statistics — no exact birthdate stored
City, State, Zip
To find nearby players and courts — no street address stored
Skill level & play preferences
To match you with compatible players
Play schedule
So others know when you like to play
What We Never Collect
✕ Street address ✕ Full date of birth ✕ Financial information ✕ Social Security number ✕ Facial images or biometrics (unless you voluntarily upload a profile photo with consent) ✕ Health records
How We Protect Your Data
Your data is stored on Supabase, which is SOC 2 Type 2 certified and built on AWS infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Phone numbers are encoded before storage. We use row-level security to restrict database access. We never share your data with third parties except as required to operate the app (e.g. sending emails via EmailJS).
Your Rights
You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing [email protected]. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
Contact
Questions about this policy? Email us at [email protected]. We're real people — you'll get a real response.
