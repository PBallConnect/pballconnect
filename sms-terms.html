SMS Consent & Terms

Information about the PBallConnect text messaging program

How You Opt In

During registration at pballconnect.com/app.html, players see the following unchecked checkbox:

I agree to receive match invites, reminders, and updates from PBallConnect by text message. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help.

By checking this box you provide express written consent for PBallConnect to send automated text messages to the number provided. Consent is not a condition of registration or use of the service.

The checkbox is unchecked by default. Opt-in requires an explicit action by the player.
